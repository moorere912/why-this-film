Why This Film?

Why This Film? is a podcast where we sit down with a guest, pick a movie that means something to them, and talk about why. Sometimes that means digging into the history of the Criterion Collection. Sometimes it's a film that shaped how someone sees the world. Either way, the goal is the same: a real conversation about a film worth talking about.

Each episode pairs a film with a guest who has something genuine to say about it — a critic, a filmmaker, a teacher, a fan. We talk about what the film does, why it still matters, and what it was like encountering it for the first time (or the fifth).
